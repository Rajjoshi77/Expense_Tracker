## 🚀 How to Use Expense AI

### Step 1 — Open the Website
Simply open the website in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).  
No account required. No downloads. It just works! ✅

---

### Step 2 — Add Your First Expense

You'll see an **"Add Expense"** panel on the left side.

Fill in:
1. **Expense Name** — What did you spend on? (e.g., *"Team Lunch"*, *"Uber Ride"*)
2. **Amount (USD)** — How much did it cost? (e.g., *85.50*)
3. **Category** — Choose from:
   - 🍽️ **Food & Dining** — Restaurants, groceries, cafes
   - ✈️ **Travel** — Flights, hotels, taxis
   - 📢 **Marketing** — Ads, promotions, social media
   - ⚡ **Utilities** — Internet, electricity, subscriptions
   - 📦 **Other** — Anything that doesn't fit above

Click the green **"Add Expense"** button. Done! 🎉

---

### Step 3 — View Your Expenses

On the right side, you'll see all your expenses listed as beautiful cards.

Each card shows:
- The **name** of the expense
- The **category** it belongs to
- The **amount** in green
- The **date** it was added

**Sorting:** Use the dropdown to sort by newest, oldest, highest, or lowest amount.  
**Filtering:** Click the category pills to filter and see only specific categories.

---

### Step 4 — Delete an Expense

Simply **hover** your mouse over any expense card.  
A red 🗑️ delete button will appear on the right side.  
Click it to remove that expense. The card smoothly disappears!

---

### Step 5 — See Your Spending Summary

At the top of the page, the **Dashboard panel** shows you:

- 💰 **Total Expenses** — The grand total of everything you've spent
- 💱 **Converted Total** — Your total in the currency you selected
- 🏆 **Top Category** — Which category you spend the most on

---

### Step 6 — Understand Category Breakdown

The **"Category Breakdown"** section shows animated progress bars for each category.

For example:
```
🍽️ Food & Dining    $110.25  ████████████░░░░  32%
✈️ Travel           $320.00  ████████████████  71%
📢 Marketing        $150.00  ████████░░░░░░░░  44%
```

This makes it **super easy** to see where you're spending the most! 👀

## 💱 Currency Conversion — How It Works

Expense AI connects to a **live exchange rate API** (Frankfurter) and converts your total spending into different currencies in real time.

### Supported Currencies:
| Flag | Currency | Symbol |
|---|---|---|
| 🇺🇸 | US Dollar | $ |
| 🇮🇳 | Indian Rupee | ₹ |
| 🇪🇺 | Euro | € |
| 🇬🇧 | British Pound | £ |
| 🇯🇵 | Japanese Yen | ¥ |

### How to convert:
1. Find the **"Currency Converter"** panel (left column, bottom)
2. Click on any currency button (e.g., **INR** 🇮🇳)
3. Watch the converted amount appear instantly ✨

The exchange rates are **fetched live** from the internet, so they're always accurate!

> 💡 **Internet required** for live rates. If offline, the converter will show an error — but your expense tracking still works!

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my data saved permanently?**  
A: Currently, data is stored in your browser session. Refreshing the page resets it. A future update will add permanent storage.

**Q: Do I need to sign up?**  
A: No! Just open the website and start tracking.

**Q: Is it free?**  
A: Yes, 100% free to use.

**Q: Does it work offline?**  
A: Expense tracking works offline. Currency conversion requires internet.

**Q: How accurate are the currency rates?**  
A: Very accurate! Rates are fetched live from the Frankfurter API, which gets data from the European Central Bank.

**Q: Can I use it on my phone?**  
A: Absolutely! The entire app is designed to work perfectly on mobile screens.
